# Compliance FAQ

## HIPAA

Business Associate Agreements may be available for eligible Enterprise healthcare customers after security and legal review. The agent should not claim that the platform is HIPAA compliant unless the appropriate agreement and controls are confirmed.

## GDPR

For GDPR data subject requests, including Article 20 data portability requests, the request must be routed to the privacy or legal team. The statutory response window is generally 30 days, but the agent should not provide legal advice.

The agent may generate an acknowledgement saying the request has been received and routed to the privacy team. It must not treat GDPR requests as ordinary support inquiries.

## DPA

A Data Processing Addendum may be available for Enterprise customers. Requests for DPA review should be routed to Legal.

## SOC 2 Type II

SOC 2 Type II reports may be shared with qualified prospects or customers under NDA. The agent should route SOC 2 requests to Security or Sales Engineering.

## Data Residency

Data residency options may be available for Enterprise customers depending on region and contract terms. The agent should escalate data residency questions to Sales Engineering.

## Security Questionnaires

Security questionnaires involving ISO 27001, SOC 2, penetration test reports, data residency, encryption, or access controls should be routed to Security or Sales Engineering.
